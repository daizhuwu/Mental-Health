Data.

Mental health problems are prevalent.
1/4 young adults between the ages of 18 and 24 have a diagnosable mental illness.
25 % of college students have been diagnosed or treated for a mental health condition within the past year.
40 % of college students have felt more than an average amount of stress within the past 12 months.
80% of college students felt overwhelmed in the past year and 45% have felt things were hopeless.
